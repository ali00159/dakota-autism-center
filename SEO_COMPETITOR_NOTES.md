# Minnesota ABA SEO Research Notes

This note captures a quick SERP snapshot used for Phase 5 optimization.

## Queries reviewed

- `ABA therapy Minnesota`
- `in-home ABA therapy Minnesota`
- `EIDBI services Minnesota`

## Competitors repeatedly visible

- `fraser.org`
- `goldenstepsaba.com`
- `abilityavenues.com`
- `familyfirst-aba.com`
- `allianceautism.org`
- `raisingstarsautism.com`
- `actionbehavior.com`

## SERP pattern summary

- Title formulas heavily frontload location + service (`ABA Therapy in Minneapolis`, `In-Home ABA Therapy in Minnesota`).
- Most descriptions emphasize practical trust signals: insurance acceptance, age ranges, BCBA-led care, and quick-start language.
- EIDBI pages that rank well clarify eligibility criteria early (under 21, diagnosis, CMDE, Minnesota program enrollment).
- In-home service pages perform better when they include family-benefit language (`in familiar environment`, `parent coaching`) instead of generic clinical phrasing only.

## Opportunity notes for Dakota

- Keep location modifiers in every service title/description, while avoiding cannibalization with city pages.
- Keep `EIDBI` as a primary keyword on the `/eidbi` page and reinforce CMDE + eligibility in metadata and schema.
- Continue emphasizing differentiators in SERPs: culturally responsive care, family-centered planning, and multi-setting coordination.
- Keep CTA language in descriptions (`get started`, `contact intake team`, `verify coverage`).
